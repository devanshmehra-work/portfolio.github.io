My Project

Store Operations Data Analysis

This project analyses store operations data to understand business performance across different suburbs. The dashboard focuses on total sales, store profit, labour percentage, waste percentage, and suburb-level comparisons.

The purpose of this project is to show how operational data can support better business decisions by highlighting which locations perform well, where costs may be high, and how key performance indicators compare against targets.

  • Analysed total sales by suburb and store location.
  • Compared store profit with total sales performance.
  • Tracked labour percentage and waste percentage against targets.
  • Created a visual dashboard suitable for business decision-making.
